### Production Notes for the Editor:
* **Text Styles:** Use native platform fonts (TikTok/Instagram standard font) with a slight colored background box to ensure readability.
* **Sound Effects:** Add a subtle "swish" sound effect during the jump cut at 0:04 and a "ding" sound effect when the text "5 min = de-puffed" appears at 0:17.
* **Music:** Layer a trending, low-volume, upbeat lo-fi track underneath the voiceover.
